About Our Company


Investment incubator is a possible specialized agency for the investment and finance sector and a leading link with many local and international experts and consultants and cooperative companies as a selection of qualified international expertise houses.


Investment incubator provides all services and facilities to implement promising projects and ideas in addition to providing international training programs and consultations necessary to achieve the goal of excellence

Our Mission

To be a reliable body in creating investment opportunities as a unique entity unlike any other and to see in its idea and goals to the needs of the targeted entities

Our Vision

Contribute to improving the investment environment and activities associated with supporting sustainability

our Goals

Economic Incubator

Establish sustainability of investments in accordance with the Kingdom's Vision 2030

Creating a business environment to serve investors by increasing competitiveness, investments and access to markets with promising export opportunities

Resource development, expansion of investments, and provision of more value-added services

Improving the mental image in the eyes of the public internally and externally, and setting up and sponsoring many forums, forums and events

Adopt new quality ideas and projects and participate in development

Contributing to the transfer of international experiences in the Kingdom and organizing meetings and events for delegations

Contributing to marketing the Kingdom as an attractive environment for investment

Improving the investment environment and improving the quality of life

Definition of available investment opportunities and overcoming barriers to their launch

Contribute to providing specialized studies in financing

Contribute to the qualification of members and companies to obtain requirements that develop from their resources

Building a partnership with government agencies, the private sector and donors
